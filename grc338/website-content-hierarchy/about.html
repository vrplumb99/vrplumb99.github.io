About Us

It all began with one woman’s quest for the world’s finest sandwich…

Daphne decided to stop looking for the world’s finest sandwich... and set out to create it instead. In 1996, she opened Daphne’s Delicious Deli with husband Richard. Now, more than two decades later, Daphne’s Delicious Deli is still delivering the best subs in town!

What makes a Daphne’s sub so good? It's the ingredients! High quality meats and cheese sliced in front of you, store baked bread and, the authentic taste – served Daphne’s Way, mayo, mustard, pickles, lettuce, tomato, onion, with our signature sauce!

Your search for the finest sandwhich is over!
